Change Oil

Changing your car's oil at regular intervals is a vital part of keeping your engine running properly. The purpose of engine oil is to keep the internal parts of your car's engine lubricated and cool. It keeps the moving parts from grinding against each other causing wear and damage.

You should get your oil changed at a lube shop or dealership (or change it yourself!) every 3,000 to 5,000 miles.
