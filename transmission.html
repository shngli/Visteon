Check Transmission

The automatic transmission's primary job is to allow the engine to operate in its narrow range of speeds while providing a wide range of output speeds.

Automatic transmission problems can cause a lack-of-response from the car, which will usually manifest while shifting into "Park" or "Drive." The car should shift quickly into either of these modes, but if your transmission hesitates to go into either one, then it's likely there's an issue to be checked out.
